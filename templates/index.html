What is a Hackathon?

A hackathon is a competition, usually 24 hours, in which students passionate about creating new technology form teams and compete with other teams to generate, develop and/or implement a new idea from scratch in order to learn and have fun.

How will I get there?

If you are commuting by train, the stop is Newark Penn Station. Then take the Light Rail towards Grove Street to Washington Street (2 Stops). Note that we will not be able to offer buses or travel reimbursments at this time.

What should I bring?

Bring whatever you need to hack. This includes your laptop, charger for your devices, and any other hardware you might need. Please also bring some form of identification. Note that we do have a list of things not to bring.

Can I work with others?

We encourage everyone to work in a team of not more than 4 people. But if you want to hack alone you are absolutely welcome to do so.
